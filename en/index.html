How it works

A virtual office without handing over your master file.

The user keeps their data. The application only opens, decrypts, edits and saves again when the user decides.

The idea is simple: load a file, work, export, close the session, clean the browser and keep your own copy outside the platform.

When a server is needed, it should be minimal: publishing, optional sync, payment or protected backup when applicable. Not the mandatory warehouse of the client's private life.

Planned products Privacy principles

Base flow

Products designed for sensitive data, documents, small businesses and users who do not want to depend on a closed cloud.

1. Open
The user uploads their JSON file or protected vault from their own device.
2. Work
The virtual office allows editing, organizing, generating documents and reports.
3. Save
A new protected or signed copy is exported, ready for USB, disk or private backup depending on the specific product.
4. Leave
The session is locked, the browser is cleaned and no central database is left exposed.

Practical privacy: fewer promises, more real control over the file.
