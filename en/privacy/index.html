Data collected

The contact form may collect name, email address, project type, message and confirmation that the privacy policy has been accepted. In addition, the web hosting provider may process basic technical data such as IP address, user agent, access date and time, requested URL and security logs needed to operate the site.

Purposes and legal basis

The data is used to answer the query, understand the project context, prepare a proposal where appropriate and maintain reasonable communication linked to that request. The main legal basis is consent. In later stages, if contracting exists, contract performance and legal obligations may apply and must be documented before continued services are provided.

Providers and transfers

The public hosting detected is GitHub Pages. The current form prepares an email through the user's email client and does not send data to a form platform from the browser. If an external provider is added, it must be identified before use.

No Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Hotjar, Plausible, Umami, YouTube embeds, Google Maps or external analytics/advertising scripts have been detected on the main website. If providers involving international transfers are added in the future, this policy will be updated and the corresponding safeguards will be applied.

Retention

Queries will be retained for the time needed to answer, make reasonable follow-up and, where appropriate, prepare a proposal. When the data is no longer needed, it should be deleted or archived according to minimization criteria and applicable obligations.

Rights

You may request access, rectification, erasure, objection, restriction, portability and withdrawal of consent by writing to gpmx@gmx.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency: www.aepd.es.

Security measures

BMB Digital Labs works with a privacy-by-design approach, data control and reasonable technical and organizational measures. It does not promise absolute security, guaranteed total privacy or end-to-end encryption unless this is implemented and documented in a specific product.
